Gauge Variant Error Metrics

Some common error metrics derived from the estimated gate set. These quanties are gauge-dependent, meaning they will depend on unphysical gauge degrees of freedom that are a necessary byproduct of estimating an entire gate set at once (akin to a freedom of reference frame). After finding a best-estimate based on the (physical) data, GST optimizes within the space of all (unphysical) gauge degrees of freedom using the parameters shown in the side bar.
